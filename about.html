About

I'm Richard Cruz — a strategic consultant, educator, and systems thinker based in the Philippines. I work with organizations navigating complex transformations: governance redesign, business model shifts, capability building, and institutional renewal.

For over 25 years, I served on the faculty of the Asian Institute of Management, where I led the School of Executive Education and Lifelong Learning. That experience — operating at the intersection of academic rigor and business reality — shaped how I think about institutional change: not as a project to be managed, but as a system to be designed.

Today, through Shift Consulting, I help leadership teams move from operational competence to genuine strategic capability. My work draws on systems thinking, complexity science, governance design, and transformative learning theory.

This site is where I think out loud — about the patterns I see, the frameworks I develop, and the questions that don't have clean answers.
